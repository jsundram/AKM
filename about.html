About

A pocket companion for the festival week: each day's schedule and weather, an offline map of the village, the roster, and a chart of who plays with whom. I built it to make it easier to see what's happening every day — and to help remember people's names.

It's a personal project, not an official festival page; the schedule here comes from the same sheets the organizers post. — Jason

Your day

Pick yourself once and the day turns personal — the warm cards are your rehearsals and private lessons, matched from the posted group assignments. ＋ add puts anything else on your timeline; those dashed cards live only on your phone. Sharing a phone, or picked the wrong name? Switch anytime from the bottom of the Today page.

If a rehearsal looks off, the source sheet ↗ link on the Today page is the ground truth.

Fresh & offline

Opening the app is the refresh — the "as of" line shows when it last pulled. Once it loads on wifi it caches the whole week, so the schedule, map, and roster keep working with no signal.

On your home screen

iPhone: open this page in Safari, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: browser menu → Add to Home screen. It opens full-screen, like an app, and works offline.

Private by design

No accounts, no server, no tracking. Who you are, the events you add, and your location on the map never leave your phone. Roster details come from the festival's participant sheet.
